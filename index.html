Your Favorite Pesto Recipe

  1. Basil leaves, 2 cups
  2. Parmesan cheese, 1/2 cup
  3. Olive oil, 1/2 cup
  4. Pine nuts, 1/3 cup
  5. Garlic cloves, 3
  6. Salt & pepper
  7. Lemon juice
  1. Pick basil leaves.
  2. Toast pine nuts.
  3. Grate cheese.
  4. Blend basil, nuts, and garlic with olive oil.
  5. Add cheese, lemon, salt, and pepper; blend.
  6. Taste and fine-tune salt, pepper, and lemon.
  7. Enjoy with preferred dishes.
